History

Rapid Adventures began with a passion for outdoor exploration and river adventures. What started as a small local rafting company has grown into one of the region's favorite outdoor experiences.

Today we guide thousands of visitors every year through exciting rapids, creating memorable experiences while promoting safety, teamwork, and a love for nature.
